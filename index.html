ALL ABOUT PERFUMES.

Perfume is a mixture of fragrant essential oils or aroma compounds (fragrances),
fixatives and solvents, usually in liquid form,used to give the human body, animals,
food, objects, and living-spaces an agreeable scent.
Perfumes can be defined as substances that emit and diffuse a pleasant and
fragrant odor.
They consist of manmade mixtures of aromatic chemicals and essential oils.The
1939 Nobel Laureate for Chemistry, Leopold Ružička stated in 1945 that
"right from the earliest days of scientific chemistry up to the present time,
perfumes have substantially contributed to the development
of organic chemistry as regards methods, systematic classification, and theory.
